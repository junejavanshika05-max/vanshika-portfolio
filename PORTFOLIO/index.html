Who I Am

I design things so people can just use them. No tutorial needed. No second guessing.

I'm Vanshika, a UI/UX designer from Delhi who somehow ended up doing her Master's in Cork, Ireland. I got into design because I genuinely find it frustrating when something should be simple and isn't. That frustration turned into a habit of pulling things apart to understand why they feel off, and then figuring out how to make them not feel that way.

My process starts way before Figma. I want to know who the person is, what they're actually trying to do, and where the current experience is failing them. That research phase is where I'm most in my element — and honestly where I think most design decisions get won or lost.

What I also bring to the table is that I can build what I design. I design and vibe code functional websites from scratch — which means I understand how design translates into real product, and I speak the same language as the developers I work with. This portfolio is proof of that.

I'm looking for a full-time role or freelance work where design is genuinely valued. If you're building something and need someone who'll think deeply before touching a single screen, let's talk.
